SURREAL LANDSCAPE

The final project we worked on before designing this website was the Surreal Landscape, combining multiple photos into one to make something surreal or just straight-up weird. This was a way to learn the more advanced mechanics & abilities of Photoshop.

I initially decided on this because it was based on my childhood wonder & almost disdain(?) of dams having been built over & flooding the beautiful land we once had, destroying the beautiful landscape that once sat underneath. This is sorta what I thought as a kid is what it looked like underwater. The sharks came later because I had a nightmare involving sharks, I took that as a sign that I needed them.

RANDOM JUNK

I make a lot of pointless and random things. Some might argue this whole site is filled with nothing but pointless and random things, but these absolutely take the cake.

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure - Adventure #01

A custom VHS tape made using the first 3 episodes of "Jojo's Bizarre Adventure - Phantom Blood". All of the video editing, such as pan & scanning & text, were done by me. The packaging design was also completely done by me. The project was a bucketlist dream ever since I was young & I was extremely happy to be able to fulfull that dream.

Steins;Gate - 001

A custom VHS tape made using the first 3 episodes of "Steins;Gate". All of the video editing, such as pan & scanning, graphics, and text, were done by me. The packaging design was also done by me with assistance of my best friend/writing partner.



Fullmetal Alchemist (2003) - Text ONE

Custom VHS covers for a hypothetical "Fullmetal Alchemist" VHS tape that I designed for the trailer found in the "Steins:Gate" tape. I haven't fully finished the packaging design, but I've at least made something that I'm pretty satisfied with.

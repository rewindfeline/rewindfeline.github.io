EXPLODED TECHNICAL ILLUSTRATION

The "Exploded Technical Illustration" was the second project I finished for the class, requiring one to take apart an object and recreate three pieces of it in illustrator in an isometric angle. I picked the Nintendo 3DS because I happened to have it in my bag the day we needed to bring an object, and it fit all the criteria.

If the previous project opened by mind to the holiness of Vector graphics, then this project completely rearranged the insides of my mind & indoctrinated me into the Vector Graphic Cult (or as we call it, the VGC). It was so much fun to do & I was blown away by the ability to easily make Isometric artwork.
